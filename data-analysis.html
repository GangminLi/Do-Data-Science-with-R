Chapter 1 Data Analysis

“Doing data science is building models.”

                                   --- David Langer

Data analysis is the key task of any data science project. It can be done with many methods depending on the goal of the analysis. The three often used methods are Descriptive analysis, Exploratory analysis and Predictive analysis as we mention in the section ?? of the chapter One. However, the dominated view of data analysis is the model building. Model building is a term used often in the predictive analysis. Building a model is to provide a simple summary of a given dataset to reflect the data “signals” or “patterns”. This summary , sometimes called mapping is formulated into a model. This model, once learnt or constructed, can then be used to predict a future trend or particular values of dependent variable.

Building prediction models to generate predictions is also called predictive analysis. It is very close the Machine Learning where a machines learns a model from the observed dataset, and after verification the model can be used for prediction of new dataset.

Human have a long history of obsessed with production. A magic crystal ball was used to predict the future.
